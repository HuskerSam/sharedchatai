    Session Discussions

      The Team

      We're a dedicated development team based in Lincoln, Nebraska, United States. We specialize in Node, Typescript, Firebase, WebGL, and AI development.

      We're always on the lookout for exciting software development projects to spur our growth and are open to collaborative opportunities. Don't hesitate to get in touch with us. Please contact us at promptplusai@gmail.com

      Unacog

      Model Agnostic

      Our platform is designed with flexibility at its core, allowing it to accommodate a variety of language models - from OpenAI to BARD, and beyond. As advancements are made in language learning models, both in capability and cost-efficiency, we're committed to providing access to these cutting-edge technologies. Currently, while we are focused on text generation tasks, our infrastructure is ready to embrace a broader range of AI-powered tasks in the future.

      Typescript and Firebase

      Our application takes inspiration from our real-time game lobby example project and harnesses the combined power of Typescript, Node, and Firebase to offer an efficient and seamless user experience.

      • User Authentication: Secure and reliable user login system.
      • Session Creation: The ability to create new sessions with customizable options.
      • Session Joining: Seamlessly join existing sessions in the lobby.
      • User Profiles: Personalize your experience with adjustable settings
      • In-session Messaging: Facilitates interaction by enabling message exchanges with other participants.
      • Real-time User Status Updates: Keep track of user activity and engagement with presence indicators.

      By structuring session and user data within Firestore documents, we ensure rapid indexing, retrieval, and consistent data handling. This robust and scalable approach, paired with Firebase's scale-on-demand hosting, offers personalized experiences, swift updates, and an integrated system that makes it an ideal choice for professionals.

      Build Philosophy

      Central to our ethos is accessibility. Our app is meticulously crafted to ensure seamless access from any device, any screen size, as long as you have a web browser. No downloads, no lengthy sign-ups, just register using your email. Simple, hassle-free, and ready whenever you are.

      • User-Centric: We design with our users in mind, striving to meet their needs, exceed their expectations, and create a seamless, intuitive experience.
      • Empowerment: We equip our users with the tools and knowledge to harness the full power of AI collaboration, enabling them to accomplish tasks more efficiently and innovatively.
      • Transparency: We believe in being clear and open about how our application works. We aim to educate users on our processes, offering them maximum control and understanding of their interactions.
      • Innovation: We continuously improve, adapt, and push boundaries to offer our users state-of-the-art functionality, staying at the forefront of AI technology.
      • Collaboration: We facilitate and promote teamwork, reflecting our belief in the combined power of collective intelligence and AI.

      Bugs, Questions, or Contact

      If you encounter any bugs, have questions, or simply want to get in touch, the best way to currently reach us is by email. We're here to help!
