Temperature influences creativity, top_p restricts diversity, presence_penalty avoids repetitive structure, and frequency_penalty minimizes repeated words or phrases. Sharing Documents and Collaborating with Others

Our AI-assisted chat document app not only allows you to collaborate with your team more effectively, it also offers many options for sharing and exporting your work. The owner of the subscription provides access to the app's key usage, enabling teams to work together on a collaborative document and produce prompt engineering results through the AI engine.

One of the most useful features of our app is the ability to export your document in text or HTML format, making it easy to share via email or other applications. This feature not only allows you to share your work with your team members, but also with individuals outside of the app who may be interested in your work.

Additionally, our app allows you to clone your existing documents and use them as the basis for new conversations. This feature ensures that you can start new chats based on previously successful prompt engineering sessions, helping you to build on your previous work and make the most of your team's collaborative power.

Finally, our app allows you to export your work to templates that can be imported by other users or future users. This makes it easy to share your prompt engineering work with individuals or teams who may not have access to the AI engine, enabling them to learn from your experiences and apply them to their own work.
