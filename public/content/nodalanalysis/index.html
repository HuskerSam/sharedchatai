do you know how to play yahtzee? can you help me keep score?

Yes, I can help you keep score in Yahtzee! In Yahtzee, each player takes turns rolling five dice and tries to obtain certain combinations to score points. The goal is to have the highest total score at the end of the game. Let's get started! First, I'll explain the scoring categories that you can choose from: 1. Ones: The sum of all ones rolled 2. Twos: The sum of all twos rolled 3. Threes: The sum of all threes rolled 4. Fours: The sum of all fours rolled 5. Fives: The sum of all fives rolled 6. Sixes: The sum of all sixes rolled 7. Three of a Kind: Total of all five dice if you have at least three of the same number 8. Four of a Kind: Total of all five dice if you have at least four of the same number 9. Full House: 25 points if you have three of a kind and a pair 10. Small Straight: 30 points for a sequence of four consecutive numbers 11. Large Straight: 40 points for a sequence of five consecutive numbers 12. Yahtzee: 50 points if all five dice show the same number 13. Chance: The sum of all the dice, you can use this category when you don't meet the requirements of any other category. To keep score, create a table with the categories listed and a corresponding column for each player. After each player's turn, simply write down their score for the chosen category in the corresponding column. At the end of the game, add up the scores in each column to determine the winner. Let me know if you need any specific assistance or if you have any questions while playing!
