Overview

Prompting gpt-3.5-turbo and chat-bison-001 to play a game of Hearts

  • Both models know the game and can play
  • They interact well with using emojis for suits
  • They played the first few rounds well
  • They failed to shuffle and deal cards
  • They failed to apply rules firmly, such as following suit
  • This turned out to be too tedious to do a best of 3 or 5 series without resorting to coding.
While the models made decent plays in early rounds they started to fail simple rules more easily as the rounds went on, making it difficult to complete a full game without making forced moves for the AI players due to them breaking rules. Hearts is probably a tough game for an AI because it's one of the few games where you don't want to take certain tricks.

Dealings hands for hearts

  • Make sure the AI shuffles the cards
  • Outputs using a concise format
  • Target 4 hands of 13 cards each
  • Watch closely for repeated cards
  • gpt-3.5-turbo is model
