Subscription for Comprehensive AI Access

Credit-base Subscription

Think of credits as currency you use to interact with AI models within the app. Each subscription plan provides a different number of credits per month, determining the number of AI interactions you can have.

Credit Consumption

Interactions with the AI models consume credits. The number of credits used depends on the length of the text (or the number of tokens) and the specific AI model used. More complex models consume more credits.

Monthly Credit Allotment

We've designed the credit allotment to suffice for most users' needs each month. However, heavy users may consume their credits faster if they frequently use the most expensive models or issue long queries.

Access to Features

All subscription plans offer unrestricted access to all features within the application. The difference lies in the number of credits that come with each plan.

Pricing Transparency

Our pricing is based on the rates set by OpenAI and other Large Language Model (LLM) API providers. We aim for transparency in our pricing, ensuring you know what you're paying for.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are credits and how do they work?

Credits are a form of currency that you use to interact with the AI models in our application. The number of credits consumed per interaction depends on the length of the text (number of tokens) and the model used. Each of our subscription plans - User, Professional, and Enterprise - provides a different number of credits per month.

What is the difference between the subscription plans?

All of our plans provide unrestricted access to our application's features. The difference lies in the number of credits they come with, which is directly proportional to the number of chat sessions you can have.

How is the pricing determined?

Our pricing is based transparently on OpenAI and other large language model (LLM) API providers. It is directly proportional to the number of tokens (length of text) and the model used in an interaction.
