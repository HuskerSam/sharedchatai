Privacy Policy

Personal Data

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. Here's how we handle your personal data:

  • Data Sharing: We will never sell or share your personal data with any third parties.
  • Data Processing: We will not view or process your personal data beyond what is necessary to render our services to you.
  • Data Deletion: When you delete a session, the data is permanently destroyed. Please note, once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
  • Email Communications: We will not use your email address for any purpose other than to provide our service to you. We will not share your email with any third parties.
  • Promotions: If you have chosen to subscribe to our email list, you will receive our promotional content in addition to tips and news. You have the right to unsubscribe at any time.
