Sharing Prompts

Unacog offers a variety of options to share your work when prompting:
  • Prompt together in realtime
  • Duplicate a session
  • Export and reuse templates
  • Use the clipboard for other applications
  • Invite people to your session using email

Import and Export Templates

Unacog sessions can be imported and exported in JSON or CSV format. These templates can be generated by external applications or edited. This is especially useful when you have a context history that is providing useful responses, and you want to keep it concise and easy to reuse while minimizing costs.

Prompt with others in realtime

You can prompt with others in real-time by sharing the session's URL or sending it via email. Anonymous login is available, as no subscription is required to use sessions with an active account. The usage charges are billed to the session owner.

Share link based access via email

Mail merge to generate emails for sharing sessions. This is very useful for using your account to supply credits (tokens) for other users, such as students or coworkers.

Share results as HTML or text

You can share the results as HTML or text by copying them to the clipboard, making it easy to share your work with other applications.

Shared session features

Shared session features include the ability to track who prompted last, see who is online and has the document open, and identify who ran which prompts. The AI can also recognize the individuals making prompts. The owner can limit the credits used by the document to keep users from spending through to many.
