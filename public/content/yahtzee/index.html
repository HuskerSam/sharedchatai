Can Chatgpt and Google Bard play a game of Hearts?
(chat-bison-001 vs gpt-3.5-turbo)

  • Both models say they know the game and can play

While the models made decent plays in early rounds they started to fail simple rules more easily as the rounds went on, making it difficult to complete a full game without making forced moves for the AI players due to them breaking rules. Hearts is probably a tougher game for the AI to play and we're going to try Spades next.

Dealings hands for hearts

  • Make sure the AI shuffles the cards
  • Outputs using a concise format
  • Target 4 hands of 13 cards each
  • Watch closely for repeated cards
