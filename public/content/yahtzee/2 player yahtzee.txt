First, I'll explain the scoring categories that you can choose from:

1. Ones: The sum of all ones rolled
2. Twos: The sum of all twos rolled
3. Threes: The sum of all threes rolled
4. Fours: The sum of all fours rolled
5. Fives: The sum of all fives rolled
6. Sixes: The sum of all sixes rolled

8. Four of a Kind: Total of all five dice if you have at least four of the same number
9. Full House: 25 points if you have three of a kind and a pair
10. Small Straight: 30 points for a sequence of four consecutive numbers
11. Large Straight: 40 points for a sequence of five consecutive numbers
12. Yahtzee: 50 points if all five dice show the same number
13. Chance: The sum of all the dice, you can use this category when you don't meet the requirements of any other category.
