To access your profile, please log in using one of the following options:

  1. Passwordless email login: Login via an email link.
  2. Google login: Sign in with your Google account.
  3. Anonymous login: Log in anonymously, but note that you won't be able to retrieve your documents if you log out.

Once you're logged in, customize your profile using:

  • Display Name and User Display Image: Add a display name and upload a profile image.
  • Preset Labels: Create labels for categorizing your documents.
  • Token Usage: View your token usage for prompt, completion, and total tokens used.
